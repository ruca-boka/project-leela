Mission

Our core goals: NEED TO GO HERE

Acoustic Data Collection

We're hoping to use our time at sea to collect valuable ocean data, focusing on acoustic data. We are in search of projects to work with. If you run a project that could benefit, we would love to hear from you.

Fundraising

We're in the process of choosing charities that will be beneficiaries of a fundraising and awareness campaign. We are prioritizing charities that work on environmental causes and/or widespread healthcare issues.

Representation

Our crossing may be the first recorded one by a pair of women of color, and we are on track to be one of the youngest pairs to attempt the crossing. Representation is close to our hearts as we hope to assure others like us that the water is a place for them.
